Table for One Trivia

Looking for something to do when it's not a Wednesday and there is no Public Works to learn from? Go to Table for One Trivia! These trivia rounds are custom-made and are held Tuesdays at Collegetown Bagels right next to Cornell and on Thursdays at Ithaca Bakery. Funny answers get you points when you don't know the correct ones!

The Downstairs

Public Works is held at The Downstairs, a local bar with a great beer selection and a regular series of events, from music to zine-making to comedy to open-mics. If you make them happy, you make us happy! Check out their upcoming events.

The Watershed

Just upstairs is The Watershed, another bar run by the same team as The Downstairs. Unlike other bars in the area, a quiet, conversational audio level is maintained. Bring your friends, have a few drinks, and actually talk to them!
