Story House Ithaca

Looking for more talks and cool community knowledge? Learn more through Story House Ithaca, regularly bringing people together to share, study, create, and enjoy stories in all their forms.

Table for One Trivia

Are you looking for something to do when it's not a Wednesday and there is no Public Works to learn from? Go to Table for One Trivia! These trivia rounds are custom-made and are held Tuesdays at Collegetown Bagels right next to Cornell and on Thursdays at Ithaca Bakery. Funny answers get you points when you don't know the correct ones!

The Downstairs & The Watershed

Public Works was held at The Downstairs, a local bar that had a great beer selection and a regular series of events, from music to zine-making to comedy to open-mics. Just upstairs was The Watershed, another bar that used to be run by the same team as The Downstairs. Sadly, both venues were closed December 2025. Please read owner Ashely Cake's closing statement.
